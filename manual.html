Field verification and deployment instructions for Ghost-Cap and Audio Ext.

Phase 1: Device Activation

01. Power On

Locate the concealed switch inside the sweatband (Ghost-Cap) or rear panel (Audio Ext). Hold for 3 seconds until the subtle LED vibrates/blinks once.

NOTE: Device enters "Stealth Mode" immediately. No visible lights will remain active during operation.
02. Charge Status

Connect via USB-C (concealed port). Solid Amber = Charging. Solid Green = Fully Charged.

Phase 2: Encrypted Uplink

01. Establish Local Link (AP Mode)

On your mobile terminal, open WiFi Settings. Search for a network named "MV..." or "TSS_SECURE..." followed by a serial ID.

Connect to this network. No password is required for initial handshake.

02. Activate Secure Viewer

Open the recommended viewer app (V380 Pro / TSS Commander). The interface will detect the local device.

03. Configure Uplink (WiFi Mode)

In the app settings, navigate to "Network" > "WiFi Station Mode". Select your local WiFi network and enter credentials.

CRITICAL: The device will reboot and connect to the internet. The local "MV..." hotspot will disappear. This indicates a successful stealth uplink.

Phase 3: Secure Stream

01. Live Monitoring

Once the uplink is established, you can access the feed securely from anywhere via the app. The stream is end-to-end encrypted.

02. Recording

Insert a FAT32 formatted MicroSD card for local loop recording, or enable Cloud Storage in the app for off-site evidence retention.
